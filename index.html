About Loopitt

Loopitt is India's premier rental marketplace, connecting people who need things with people who have them. Our mission is to make renting as easy as buying, but more affordable and sustainable.

Nationwide Presence

Operating in over 20 cities across India with a network of trusted vendors.

Quality Assurance

Every product is verified and maintained to ensure the best quality for our customers.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees or charges. Pay only for what you use, when you use it.

Founded in 2020

Serving over 100,000 customers

Our Vision

We envision a world where access trumps ownership, where people can enjoy high-quality products without the burden of buying, storing, and maintaining them. By promoting a sharing economy, we're reducing waste and making better use of resources.
